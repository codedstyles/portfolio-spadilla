Lifestyle app

Client: Traveline | Category: UI/UX Design

Overview

Our market research showed that if a person is trying to achieve a goal, the more often they monitor their progress, the greater the likelihood that they will succeed, and their chances of reaching their goals are even more likely if they physically record them. Following this premise, the idea of an all around lifestyle app was embarked. A mobile application that will present this concept in a delightful and user-friendly manner.

MVP design

The interface was presented with a minimalistic style - ensuring the user a straight forward experience. The use of bright and inviting colors are ideal choices, suggesting vitality, vibrance and evoking action. The user is able to add their information and check their progress with ease, by having consistency - in colors, typeface and design patterns. Creating a unified tone of voice that adds cohesiveness to the user experience.

With the interface defined, our approach was to build a Minimum Viable Product with a maximum of 5 features - most recommended. Then performing testing and iterations until all the necesssary elements were given the green light by the user.

Results & Implementation

By presenting a user centered design approach, an engaging User Interface, along with contextually relevant content, the MVP model proved to be the first important milestone achieved.
