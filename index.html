Mobile Design

Overview

Research shows if you are trying to achieve a goal, the more often you monitor your progress, the greater the likelihood that you will succeed, and your chances of reaching your goals are even more likely if you physically record it. Following this premise, the idea of a all around lifestyle app was proposed. A mobile application that will present this concept in an enjoyable and user-friendly way.

Approach

The interface is presented with a minimalistic direction - ensuring the user an easy and clear experience. The use of bright and inviting colors are ideal choices, suggesting vitality, vibrance and evoking action. The user is able to add their information and check their progress with ease, by having consistency - in colors, typeface and design patterns. Creating a unified tone of voice that adds cohesiveness to the user experience.

Results

By focusing on the user, delivering a personalized experience, presenting contextually relevant content and testing the design systems efficacy, the lifestyle app was a success.

Mobile Design

Overview

The first of the month comes and it is time to pay our monthly bills. Missing to pay them and suddenly we are without cable, phone, a gym membership, among other bills to pay. payBill is a mobile application that takes care of this - pay all your bills in one place and from your phone.

Approach

The interface displays a clean and simple design, streamlining the content in order to optimize users’ engagement and experience. The use of green and a grey palette bring a balancing and harmonizing effect to the interface. The design flow was established to be intuitively - for users that are on-the-go.

Results

By identifying the target market and performing user testing at the many stages of development, the right design direction was taken and the mobile application was pleseantly received. At this time it is progressing to the next phase which is adding more features and functionality for future releases.
