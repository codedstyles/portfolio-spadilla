Protiviti

Client: Protiviti | Category: Animated Banners

Testing and optimizing is imperative to any successful advertising campaign. With Protiviti I performed various rounds of testing, which provided valuable information about what worked and what didn’t for their specific target audience.

I built 3 different ads with the following elements:

  • A single distinguishable call to action
  • A single headline
  • The company’s brand name
  • A single focal point

Once we started getting significant results, I began doing split tests to better optimize the best performers. The following ads were the winners, as they got the most CTR within their corresponding campaigns.

Culture Change e-banner

The new design outperformed the control and achieved a 25% higher conversion rate.

Risk Management e-banner

The ad was relevant and personalized to the consumer, increasing 128% more visitors.
